Academic Career

After a decade of searching for that elusive "perfect" career field, I can honestly say I'm happy with my decision to go into Information Technology. I've dabbled in almost every major imaginable, from veterinary medicine to electrical engineering. At one point I felt like the Goldilocks of academic majors: "This one has too much math!" or "This one takes too long!". The ITM program at IIT turned out to be fun and the campus is close to Reggie's, one of my favorite rock bars. I can tell that students and faculty alike have a strong passion for anything tech. That impressed me the most.

Academic Achievements

I rocked my first year here as a transfer not only by being selected as a Featured ITM Student for the official IIT website, but also by winning a scholarship to the Women in Cyber Security Conference in Arizona, by designing and building a Smart Mirror that gained a decent amount of exposure, and landing a badass internship at the Exelon Power Plant in Morris, IL. So logically, if IIT thinks I'm that cool, imagine how much I would kick ass at your company.
